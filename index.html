Recruitment

Internship


We are a technology consultancy company head-quartered in Chennai with team members located around India. We have a few internship opportunities for recent engineering graduates. Current students are also welcome if their teachers/department do not object to them earning some fee and spending the time. The candidates must have demonstrable experience on GNU/Linux and have solid programming expertise in preferably C/C++. Experience in Golang would be an added advantage. The candidates must have good understanding of software development fundamentals.

The selected candidates would be extremely hard-working and driven to excel. They would be able to work well with other team members and will have every opportunity to closely interact and contribute to the open source community. The projects they work on will be chosen by matching the candidate skills with company needs.

The internship projects may lead to full-time consulting positions depending on company growth need and internship success.

If you are interested in applying for the position described above, please email your resume to interns@ldcs.co.in, with a subject line LDCS-firstname-lastname. If included, links to your FOSS work is a superior gauge of technical and team ability.
